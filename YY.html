Our Philosophy

We believe legal services should be affordable, scalable, and simple. We seek to integrate with your team and add value to the organization from day one.

How we work

From our initial consult, we seek to get a realistic idea of your objectives and your businesses needs. We craft billing arrangements and engagements that are unique to your bussiness. Whether you simply need periodic consultation, or on site support, we are ready to help you grow. We do not limit the scope of our work to legal issues. We are prepared to assist in whatever capacity you need us and we pride ourselves on being more than a law firm.

Not your father's law firm

We leave the pretentiousness and arrogance out of the legal profession. We keep our expenses reasonable. We do not dictate our emails for a secretary to transcribe. We embrace technology. We do not pay for the mahogony desk. You are welcome to come as you are.

Let's get in touch

Reach out at any time with a brief description of your needs and scheduling availability and we will get a consultation on the calendar. Send inquiries to info@younglawcolorado.com.

Outside General Counsel

We are ready to address your toughest legal questions. From day in day out questions about employment and termination, to contract review and drafting, to navigating complex regulatory issues - we are here to help.

Contract review and drafting

We believe the best way to get ahead of employment issues is to ensure your workplace has a strong culture before issues arise. We can do a comprehensive assessment of your litigation risk and make recommendations to minimize the risk of employment related litigation. If prevention is not enough, our attorneys are prepared to represent your business in any employment related dispute.
